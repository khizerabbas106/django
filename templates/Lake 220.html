Description about Lake 220

Glacier retreat results in the formation and expansion, and sometimes outburst, of moraine-dammed lakes worldwide.
Sudden outburst floods from such lakes have caused enormous damage to settlements and infrastructure located downstream.
Such lakes located in the Himalayan region are highly prone to outburst floods due to climatic conditions and geotectonic settings.
This lake is very susceptible. The past incidents show it's outbursts at various times.
